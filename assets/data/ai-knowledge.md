## CISRS scaffolding courses
All CISRS courses run 8:00am–4:00pm. Certificates and record cards are valid for 5 years. All tools, scaffolding equipment (helmets, gloves, harnesses, lanyards) and materials are provided. PPE required: steel-toe boots + appropriate work clothing/overalls.

- **CISRS OSTS Scaffolder Level 1** — 5 days — TT$8,437.50. No prior experience needed; minimum 6 months practical experience recommended. Erect, alter and dismantle basic tube-and-fitting scaffolds. Practical + theory + assessment. Certificate + record card, valid 5 years. Career path: progress to Level 2 after 6 months experience.
- **CISRS OSTS Scaffolder Level 2** — 5 days — TT$8,437.50. Requires Level 1 card held for at least 6 months. Intermediate structures: cantilevers, gantry/crash deck, crane loading bay. Certificate + record card, valid 5 years. Career path: progress to Level 3 after 6 months.
- **CISRS OSTS Scaffolder Level 3** — 5 days — TT$9,000.00. Requires Level 2 card held for at least 6 months. Complex structures: bridging, lifting frames, splay/radial. Certificate + record card, valid 5 years. Career path: progress to Level 4 after 12 months.
- **CISRS OSTS Scaffolder Level 4** — 10 days — contact office for price. Complex tube-and-fitting; qualified to supervise operatives. *(Coming 2026)*
- **CISRS OSTS System Scaffold — Cuplok** — 2 days. Requires minimum Level 1. Practical & theoretical training to erect and dismantle Cuplok system scaffolding safely. Certificate of attendance.
- **CISRS OSTS System Scaffold — Ringlock** — 2 days. Requires minimum Level 1. Same as Cuplok but for Ringlock system scaffolding.
- **CISRS OSTS Basic Scaffold Inspection** — 3 days — TT$6,750.00. For scaffolders AND managers/engineers who inspect basic scaffolds. Some scaffolding awareness/experience required. Inspect basic structures, complete inspection reports. Certificate + record card, valid 5 years. Career path: Advanced Inspection after holding Basic card for 2 years.
- **CISRS OSTS Advanced Scaffold Inspection** — 2 days — TT$6,500.00. Requires Basic Inspection card OR Level 3 card, held for at least 2 years. Complex designed structures. Certificate + record card, valid 5 years.
- **CISRS OSTS Scaffold Supervisor** — 3 days — TT$7,875.00. Requires 5 years industry experience; ideally Level 3 or 4. Supervision, direction and instruction of scaffolding operations — toolbox talks, legal duties, risk assessments. Certificate + supervisor card, valid 5 years.
- **CISRS OSTS Scaffolding Awareness** — 1 day. No specific prerequisites (basic construction/scaffolding environment understanding recommended). For directors, managers, safety advisors, supervisors who oversee scaffold operations but don't erect them. Certificate of attendance.
- All CISRS assessments: practical demonstration + written assessment (+ viva on most courses). Certification only awarded when trainee is deemed competent; resit guidance given if needed.
- **UK mapping**: OSTS cards map to equivalent UK CISRS cards with additional HS&E test and in some cases NVQ/SCQF and skills test requirements. Ask the office for details.

## Which course do I need? (career route)
- Never scaffolded before → start with CISRS Level 1 (steel-toe boots, basic fitness, head for heights — everything else provided).
- Erecting/altering/striking scaffolds professionally → CISRS route: Level 1 → Level 2 → Level 3 → Level 4.
- Run scaffold gangs and supervise → Scaffold Supervisor (needs Level 3/4 + 5 years experience).
- Sign off scaffolds as safe → Scaffold Inspection — Basic then Advanced.
- Work near scaffolds / at height but don't build them (trades, managers, HSE advisors) → Getmie Safe Working at Height.
- Teams needing emergency rescue capability → Getmie Safe Rescue: Basic → Advanced → Refresher annually.
- Oversee scaffold contractors on your site → Scaffolding Awareness (1 day, no prerequisites).

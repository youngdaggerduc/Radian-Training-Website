Our History: 25 Years of Raising Industry Standards
In the late 1990s, the introduction of international standards by multinational companies
highlighted the need for recognised, reliable training within the local market. In
response, Radian H.A. Limited was established in 2001 to provide internationally
aligned training and consultancy services to the Oil & Gas, Construction, and
Petrochemical industries.
For the past 25 years, Radian has remained committed to raising industry standards
locally and regionally through expert-led training delivered in accordance with global
best practice. Central to this commitment are our long-standing strategic partnerships.
Since our inception, we have worked closely with Safety & Access (UK) - a leading
training and health & safety consultancy with over 30 years of experience. We have also
partnered with Getmie Safe (UK) to deliver specialised Working at Height and Technical
Rescue training.
Beyond training, Radian supports major projects through the supply of scaffolding
materials and ancillaries, followed by the provision of professional tools and safety
equipment in association with Leach’s (UK), distributors of Nailspan®, IMN®, and BIG
BEN® safety products.
Our programmes are delivered in line with TG20:21, SG4:22, and BS EN 12811-1,
based on the British Standard framework incorporating international standards.
As we mark our 25th anniversary, Radian continues to deliver a trusted, one-stop
solution, training, materials, tools, and consultancy - supporting a safer, more competent
industry across Trinidad and Tobago and the wider Caribbean.

Radian is a CISRS-certified training centre, operating in accordance with a globally recognised
benchmark for scaffolding competence.
CISRS OSTS Scaffolder Level 1 | 5 days | 8am - 4pm
Provides skills to erect, alter, and dismantle basic tube-and-fitting
under appropriate supervision.
CISRS OSTS Scaffolder Level 2 | 5 days | 8am - 4pm
Provides skills to erect, alter, and dismantle intermediate tube-and-fitting
under appropriate supervision.
CISRS OSTS Scaffolder Level 3 | 5 days | 8am - 4pm
Provides skills to erect, alter, and dismantle complex tube-and-fitting
under appropriate supervision.
CISRS OSTS Scaffolder Level 4 | 10 days | 8am - 4pm
Provides skills to erect, alter, and dismantle complex tube-and-fitting,
qualified to supervise operatives.
CISRS OSTS Basic Scaffold Inspection | 3 days | 8am - 4pm
Provides delegates with the knowledge & skills to enable them to
inspect basic scaffold structures & complete safety inspection reports.
CISRS OSTS Advanced Scaffold Inspection | 2 days | 8am - 4pm
Provides delegates with the knowledge & skills to enable them to inspect
complex designed structures & complete safety inspection reports.
CISRS OSTS System Scaffold (Cuplok/Ringlock) | 2 days | 8am - 4pm
Provides delegates with practical & theoretical knowledge to erect &
dismantle cuplok or ringlock system scaffolding safely, according to current
legislation & the manufacturer’s instructions.
CISRS OSTS Scaffold Supervisor | 3 days | 8am - 4pm
Provides delegates such knowledge and skills to enable them to supervise,
monitor, direct & instruct Scaffolders in scaffolding operations.
CISRS OSTS Scaffold Awareness | 1 day | 8am - 4pm
Provides delegates with the relevant knowledge for safe assessment,
monitoring, & management of scaffolding operations to ensure efficient &
compliant works.
